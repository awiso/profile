My Favorite Places

Grundlsee, Austria

Grundlsee is a municipality in the Liezen District of Styria, Austria, which counted 1,223 inhabitants as of 2015. The municipality is located on Grundlsee lake.

New York City, USA

The City of New York, often called New York City (NYC) or simply New York, is the most populous city in the United States. With an estimated 2017 population of 8,622,698 distributed over a land area of about 302.6 square miles (784 km2), New York City is also the most densely populated major city in the United States.

Berlin, Germany

Berlin is the capital and the largest city of Germany, as well as one of its 16 constituent states. With a steadily growing population of approximately 3.7 million, Berlin is the second most populous city proper in the European Union behind London and the seventh most populous urban area in the European Union.
